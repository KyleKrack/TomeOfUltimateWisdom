Basic Game Rules

Every player takes turns moving their character and making actions. The dice system is used for resolving combat and skill checks.

Turn Order

Each player rolls a d20 at the start of combat to determine their place in the turn order.

Skill Checks

Skill checks are rolled using a d20. Add the relevant skill modifier to the roll to determine success.
