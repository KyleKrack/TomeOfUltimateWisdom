Captain Roderick of Aradorn

Role: Leader of the Aradornian City Guard

Description: A grizzled veteran, Captain Roderick has seen many battles and prefers peace over war. He is stern but fair and will offer quests to adventurers who earn his trust.

Rumor: "They say Captain Roderick has a map to the lost catacombs under the city..."

Mira, the Wandering Merchant

Role: Trader of Exotic Goods

Description: Mira travels between the Free Isles and the Kingdom of Aradorn. She is always looking for rare items to trade, and her prices are known to fluctuate depending on demand.

Rumor: "Mira claims to have a magical compass that points to treasure. But can she be trusted?"

Kelron the Alchemist

Role: Master of Potions and Alchemy

Description: Kelron is a reclusive alchemist living on the outskirts of the Shimmering Desert. His potions are said to work wonders, but they come at a steep price.

Rumor: "Some say Kelron’s potions are more than they seem, laced with ancient magic."

Sylva, Priestess of the Forest

Role: Keeper of the Ancient Groves

Description: Sylva is the last of the druids who guard the sacred forests. She is deeply connected to the land and will not hesitate to curse those who harm nature.

Rumor: "It's said that Sylva can communicate with the forest spirits to guide lost travelers."
