Alchemy and Crafting Recipes

Combine items to create powerful potions and gear:

  • Healing Potion: 1 Herb + 1 Water = Restores 10 HP.
  • Strength Elixir: 2 Rare Mushrooms + 1 Gemstone = +2 Strength for 1 hour.
  • Fire Bomb: 1 Oil + 1 Gunpowder = Explodes on impact, dealing fire damage.

Spellbooks

Specialize in different schools of magic by finding these spellbooks:

  • Pyromancer's Tome: Contains fire-based spells like Fireball, Flame Shield, and Ignite.
  • Necromancer's Grimoire: Dark magic, including Raise Skeleton, Curse, and Soul Drain.
  • Illusionist's Manual: Master illusions with spells like Invisibility, Mirror Image, and Phantasmal Killer.
