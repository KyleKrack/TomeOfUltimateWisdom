Pixel Art

An introduction to the art of creating pixel-perfect designs for games.

Pixel art is a digital art form where images are created and edited at the pixel level. It’s commonly used in retro-style games.

  • Aseprite
  • Pyxel Edit
  • Photoshop or GIMP

Mastering pixel art requires attention to detail. Start small, learn about color palettes, and focus on key elements like shading and outlines.

Pixel art can set the visual tone for a game. It’s often used in indie games and retro revivals to create a nostalgic feel.
