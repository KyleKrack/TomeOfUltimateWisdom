Culture and Society

Arlathor is a world of ancient kingdoms, filled with magic and mystery. The people are divided into several major factions:

  • The Taliaan Empire: A militaristic society, prizing honor and conquest.
  • The Elven Kingdoms: Ruled by wise councils, the elves value knowledge and magic.
  • The Free Cities: A loose coalition of traders and merchants, known for their independence.
