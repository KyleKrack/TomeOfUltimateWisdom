Step 1: Choose Your Race

Select one of the following races for your character:

  • Human: +1 to all stats.
  • Elf: +2 Dexterity, -1 Constitution.
  • Dwarf: +2 Constitution, -1 Charisma.
  • Orc: +2 Strength, -1 Intelligence.

Step 2: Assign Attributes

Distribute points among your character's attributes (Strength, Dexterity, Constitution, Intelligence, Wisdom, and Charisma). You have 10 points to distribute. Each stat must be between 1 and 5.

Step 3: Choose a Class

Your class determines your abilities and skills:

  • Fighter: Specializes in melee combat and heavy armor.
  • Wizard: Masters of magic and spells.
  • Rogue: Stealthy, agile, and good at lockpicking.
  • Cleric: Healers with divine magic.

Step 4: Background

Choose a background for your character. This defines their past and skills they gained:

  • Noble: +2 to Charisma, access to wealth and influence.
  • Mercenary: +2 to Strength, proficient in weapons.
  • Scholar: +2 to Intelligence, access to ancient knowledge.

Step 5: Equipment

Start with basic equipment such as a weapon, armor, and adventuring gear based on your class.
