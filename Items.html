Common Items

In the game, players will come across various items that they can use on their adventures. Here are some common items:

  • Health Potion: Restores 10 HP.
  • Rope: Useful for climbing or tying things.
  • Torch: Lights the way in dark places.
  • Lockpicks: Helps in opening locked doors and chests.

Magical Items

Magical items are rare and powerful. They can significantly enhance your character's abilities:

  • Ring of Invisibility: Grants temporary invisibility for 1 minute.
  • Fire Staff: Casts fireball spells. Deals 2d6 fire damage.
  • Elven Boots: Provides +2 to Dexterity rolls.
